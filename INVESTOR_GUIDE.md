## üí° INVESTOR TALKING POINTS

### 1. **Platform Business Model**
"Unlike single-sided health apps, Sana creates a three-sided marketplace where each user type adds value to the others."

### 2. **Data Moat**
"Every patient interaction generates valuable health data. Researchers pay for access, creating a data flywheel that funds patient subsidies."

### 3. **Multi-Sided Revenue**
- **Patients:** Freemium model ($0-15/month)
- **Practitioners:** SaaS subscription ($50-200/month)
- **Researchers:** Data licensing ($1K-10K+ per dataset)

### 4. **Scalability**
"Built on Expo/React Native (mobile) and React Router (web) for rapid cross-platform deployment. Single codebase, multiple platforms."

### 5. **Regulatory Pathway**
"Starting with wellness/lifestyle (low regulatory burden), then expanding to clinical features as we scale."

### 6. **Go-to-Market**
- **Phase 1:** Launch patient app with content/marketplace
- **Phase 2:** Onboard practitioners in pilot cities
- **Phase 3:** Open researcher platform with first datasets
